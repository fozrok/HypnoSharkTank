## Here's How It Works

You answer a few questions about your experience, your skills, and what you enjoy working on. Then CoachSharkTank runs you through **7 steps** — each one building on the last:

1. **Niche Finder** — It analyses your profile and ranks your top 6 niche opportunities by revenue potential, scalability, and fit. No guesswork.

2. **Model Builder** — It turns your chosen niche into a complete business model: offer design, pricing, delivery format, and positioning.

3. **Market Validator** — It stress-tests your idea against real market data. You get a Green Light, Pivot, or Red Flag verdict — so you're not building something nobody wants.

4. **Revenue Projector** — A 12-month financial model with conservative, base, and aggressive scenarios. You'll see exactly when you hit profitability.

5. **Messaging Engine** — It writes your content hooks, outreach sequences, and trust-building language. Ready to copy-paste into your marketing.

6. **Launch Sprint** — A 30-day, day-by-day execution plan. Week 1: set up. Week 2: soft launch. Week 3: optimize. Week 4: scale. With KPIs so you know if you're on track.

7. **Reality Check** — The final audit. It flags failure patterns, missed opportunities, and stall risks — so you can fix them *before* you launch.
